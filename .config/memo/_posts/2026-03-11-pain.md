# Wed 11 Mar 2026 17:20:40 EDT

I feel like one of the main issues as to why people do not do the projects they want to do, do not have good personal projects, or aren't getting anywhere in life, is not necessarily that they don't know what to do. A lot of people know what to do. The problem is that they don't invest their time right, and they live their life on autopilot. When you live your life on autopilot, you default to autopilot.
You have all of these things:

- easy things like scrolling
- watching YouTube videos
- being on Facebook
  You miss out on more intentional things, like sitting down, looking at your life, and thinking: "Okay, what are some things in my life that I do not like? That I would like to be different? Am I living my best life right now?"
