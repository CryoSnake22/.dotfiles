## MVP phase:

### Part 1 : Find your early adopters

- Get people's attention to get them on a call.
- Give them a 5 minute pitch
- Give them a great offer to try to get them to pay you something for your solution.
- My idea is give two options,
  - give them access to early access beta with limited credits
  - or give them unlimited credits for a discounted price
- Get them to agree to give regular feedback for a free subscription

### Part 2 : questions to ask people:

- Do you understand the problem I'm trying to solve
- Do you have the problem I'm trying to solve
- How are you solving this problem today
- Would you pay for this solution if I could get it to you tomorrow.
- What else would this solution have to do in order for you to pay for it
- I'm thinking of charging ($$), would you pay that price ?

### MVP Step 1

- Build as little as possible as quickly as possible to get your product in people's hands.
- Don't sink any time or money developing features you don't need.

### MVP Step 2

- Focus on the smallest possible niche (CS students and new grads)

### MVP Step 3:

- Set up automated outreach.
- Run an onboarding call with each user (Get immediate insight and get an ongoing feedback machine in the future) zoom call, in person anything.
- Once that's done setup automated sequence

```
Hey [Name] ,
Just checking in on [APPLICATION],
How's the product going ?
Are you seeing [VALUE YOU ARE TRYING TO DELIVER]?
```

- Then use AI tool to whip up 4-6 variations of the message and schedule them to go out over the next 8 to 12 weeks.

Boomerang, gmass, etc.

### MVP Step 4:

- Iterate the product
- Get more people to pay you
- Try to get referrals from happy customers

Write down all the feedback in a spreadsheet
Record value of the users requesting the features
Record frequency of request in other column
Estimated time to build and implement

### MVP Step 5:

- Keep your eyes open for magic moments
- During every interaction look for overjoyed reactions and happy reactions.

If I took my product away from my users how upset would they get ?

### MVP Step 6:

- Keep adding beta users by:
  - Asking for referrals
  - Posting on social media
  - Getting on relevant podcasts
  - Comment on relevant social media people
  - Connect with people on linkedin
  - Go to industry events, ask people if they would buy if not ask why
  - Ask your friends and colleagues on linkedin
